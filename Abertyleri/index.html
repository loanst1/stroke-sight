About Stroke Sight

About this app

Stroke Sight is a wellness support app for people living with hemianopia — partial vision loss commonly caused by stroke. It is not a medical device and does not replace professional rehabilitation. Use it alongside guidance from your neuro-optometrist or stroke rehab team.

Why these four exercises?

After a stroke causing hemianopia, the brain loses automatic scanning into the affected field. These exercises are based on compensatory saccadic training — teaching deliberate eye movements to compensate for the missing field.

The exercises

  • Anchor & Scan — 70% of targets appear on the affected side, forcing deliberate saccades (eye jumps) into the blind field.
  • Reading Line Scan — rebuilds the anchor-scanning reading habit disrupted by stroke. A red dot marks the start of each line.
  • Peripheral Flicker — low-demand awareness training. Stay fixed on the cross and react to yellow flickers. Good for fatigue days.
  • Visual Search Grid — simulates real tasks (scanning a menu, finding items on a shelf) requiring full-field systematic searching.

How to use this app

  • Aim for 5–15 minutes per day, little and often is better than long infrequent sessions.
  • Start with Peripheral Flicker if fatigue is high.
  • Show your Progress tab to your therapist at appointments.
  • This app works fully offline — no internet needed after first save.

Important

This app is a supplement to professional rehabilitation, not a replacement. Recovery from stroke-related visual field loss varies greatly between individuals. Always follow your clinical team's guidance.

The short version

Stroke Sight collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no internet required.

Nature of this application

Stroke Sight is a wellness support application, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, monitor, or cure any medical condition. It is intended as a self-directed home practice aid to complement professional stroke rehabilitation care.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Specifically:

  • No name, email, or contact information
  • No health or medical data transmitted anywhere
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • No crash reports or diagnostics

On-device storage

Your exercise progress (scores, reaction times, session history) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device, is not backed up to any server, and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Third-party services

Stroke Sight contains no third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, social media integrations, or cloud services of any kind.

Children’s privacy

Stroke Sight is suitable for all ages. We do not collect data from any user, including children. The app inherently complies with COPPA, the UK Children’s Code, and GDPR provisions for children’s data.
